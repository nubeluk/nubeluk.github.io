What is

Huxley is a digital platform, designed to allow fundraising for organisations and charities to manage their own skill based competitions. Take the burden and cost out of developing and running your own platform.

Choose your platform plan

Choose the right plan for your business needs. Huxley allows you to grow as your competition requirements increase. All plans allow unlimited players, give you the choice to set your own ticket prices, the number of tickets available and the prizes you want to give away.
