The Legal, Low-Cost Draw Solution

Huxley provides a complete framework that takes the complexity out of charity draws, giving you all the tools to engage your base and generate sustainable revenue.

Fast Set-Up

Skip the development cycle. Huxley is ready to launch out of the box, letting you start your first competition in days rather than months.

No Gambling Licence Needed

Huxley is built around skill-based competitions, meaning you do not have to undergo complex, expensive, and restrictive licensing procedures.

Activity Reporting

Deep dive into player stats, ticket volumes, and fundraising totals. Clean dashboards give you real-time visibility into your growth.

Designed Specifically for Charities

We understand the hurdles charities face when running draws. Huxley is designed from the ground up to solve them.

Open Banking Lower Rates

Card payment gateways eat into charity profits with high transaction fees. Huxley integrates Open Banking to process player tickets. This yields dramatically lower transaction rates, instant settlement, and improved safety, ensuring more money goes directly to your cause.

Unlimited Players & Custom Pricing

No artificial ceilings on your success. All plans allow you to host unlimited players. Set your own ticket prices, designate how many tickets are available, and offer custom prizes that appeal directly to your target community.

Secure API & Custom Integrations

Every Huxley plan includes secure API access. Integrate your fundraising draws into existing websites and databases automatically.

Step 01

Choose a Plan in Minutes

Pick the subscription tier that matches your campaign scale. From lightweight self-managed setups to fully managed options, we have a plan for every organisation size. Simply contact our team to speak with an agent and begin the onboarding process.

Step 02

Configure Your Brand Identity

Provide your logo, brand colours, and primary site copy. Our onboarding team applies your styling onto your dedicated Huxley account. Keep your brand front and centre for donor trust.

Step 03

Set Up Your Competitions

Select your competition rules, upload photos of the prizes, configure ticket costs, and write your compliance question. Simply add our code snippet to your existing website to bring the competitions to life, then hit launch to make the competition instantly public.

Step 04

Direct Banking & fundraising Growth

Watch registrations roll in. Open Banking directs payments securely into your organisation's bank accounts with minimal overhead fees. Generate detailed audits at draw time and announce your winners!
